Modern Guestbook

A fun and easy digital guestbook

Create Your Guestbook

Impress your guests

Modern Guestbook lets your guests "sign" your guestbook by simply taking a picture on their phone and writing a post.

Easy set up. No technology required

Modern Guestbook simply requires you to display a good old sign at your event. It doesn't require any stands, iPads, cameras or microphones.

Simple and Reliable

Because Modern Guestbook uses the power of your guests' cell phones, it is easy for them and reliable for you. We know you have enough to worry about.

How it works

Display your custom guestbook sign

We'll custom design the sign for your event and send you a digital copy. The sign contains a QR code that is unique to your event and will direct your guests to your event's page.

Guests scan the QR code

When guests point their camera at the QR code on the sign, it will open your event's page where guests can sign you book by making a post and uploading a photo.

Snap a pic and post a message

Once they are on your event's page, guests take a picture and leave you a heartfelt message. They can even upload an old picture with a fun memory!
